About Us

Princeton Learning Experience Hong Kong is a student-led nonprofit organization dedicated to providing free, high-quality online English tutoring to students across Hong Kong and Shenzhen. Founded by passionate students, we believe that language should never be a barrier to opportunity.

Our mission is simple: connect talented tutors with eager learners in a supportive, engaging environment. Through personalized one-on-one sessions, we help students build confidence, improve their English proficiency, and unlock new academic possibilities.

Tutoring Schedule: Sessions are held every Friday, Saturday, and Sunday from 8:00-8:40 PM HKT, providing flexible weekend learning opportunities that fit around your schedule.
